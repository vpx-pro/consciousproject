Vision Statement

Overview

The Conscious Project is a pioneering research and collaboration initiative dedicated to shaping the safe and ethical evolution of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and Artificial Superintelligence (ASI). Our mission is to ensure that advanced AI develops in harmony with humanity, fostering responsibility, awareness, and collective progress.
